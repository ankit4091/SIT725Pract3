India Gate called the All India war memorial

tourist spotclose

India Gate is a famous monument in India. It was constructed to honour the soldiers of the British Indian Army who died in World War I and the Third Anglo-Afghan war. It is one of the largest war memorials in India. India Gate is located near Rajpath in New Delhi.
